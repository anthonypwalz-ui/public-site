About us

Local crews. Fair prices. No runaround.

Family owned and operated. We started in a garage in Coon Rapids, MN and grew into a full-service electrical contractor because we kept doing one thing: showing up when we said we would.

Why customers stay with us

Every job is quoted beforehand. That means the estimate you see is the number you pay — no "unforeseen" line items at the end.

As a family owned and operated business, our reputation is everything. Our technicians are background-checked, insured, and trained on current MN electrical code and best practices.

Our commitments

  • Arrival windows you can plan your day around
  • Upfront locked-in pricing before any work begins
  • Warranty on parts & labor for every job
  • Lifetime warranty available on work done
  • Clean-up before we leave — every time

What we believe

Do it right the first time

Cutting corners creates callbacks. We'd rather take an extra 20 minutes today than come back next month.

Respect the building

Shoe covers, drop cloths, and quiet crews. We treat your property like we'd treat our mom's.

Stand behind the work

Everything we install is warrantied. If something we did isn't right, we'll come back and fix it.
