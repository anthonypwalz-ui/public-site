Privacy policy

Short version: we only collect what we need to help, and we don't sell it.

What we collect

When you submit a form on this site we collect your name, contact info, location, and any project details you provide. Our server logs may also record IP address and browser info for security purposes.

How we use it

To reply to your request, schedule service, send invoices or estimates, and if you opt in to follow up on future maintenance needs. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

Who we share it with

We share data only with service providers that help us operate (email delivery, payment processing, scheduling software) and only as needed to perform those services.

Your choices

Email office@BoldNorthElectric.com any time to review, correct, or delete the information we have about you.
